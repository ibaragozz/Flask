Blog about Soviet Cosmonauts

This section provides a collection of articles about legendary Soviet cosmonauts, their missions, and achievements in space exploration.

Yuri Gagarin - First Man in Space

On April 12, 1961, Yuri Gagarin became the first human to journey into outer space aboard Vostok 1. His mission marked a pivotal point in the space race.

Alexei Leonov - First Spacewalk

On March 18, 1965, Alexei Leonov became the first person to perform a spacewalk, stepping out of his Voskhod 2 spacecraft into the vacuum of space.

Valentina Tereshkova - First Woman in Space

On June 16, 1963, Valentina Tereshkova became the first woman to fly into space aboard Vostok 6, inspiring generations of women around the world.
